The problem today

Businesses track critical renewals with spreadsheets, sticky notes, and hope.

Restaurants, contractors, childcare centers, salons, clinics, and other location-based businesses often juggle dozens of licenses and permits across different dates, jurisdictions, and forms.

Sticky notes and memory
A manager writes renewal dates on paper or keeps them in someone’s head until that person leaves or forgets.
Spreadsheet chaos
Files live in disconnected spreadsheets with no centralized visibility across locations, owners, or permit types.
Expensive mistakes
One missed renewal can trigger fines, temporary closure, delayed openings, or even loss of contracts.
